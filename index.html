About Us

We are a student‑run program dedicated to trial advocacy at Bridgewater State University. Teams prepare both sides of a single case each season and compete under American Mock Trial Association (AMTA) rules.

What Mock Trial Teaches

  • Courtroom presence and professional demeanor
  • Clear, structured argumentation under time limits
  • Strategic thinking & case theory
  • Questioning techniques for direct and cross
  • Evidence handling & objections
  • Teamwork and discipline
