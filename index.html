What We Do

A disciplined courtroom simulation: opening statements, examinations, objections, and closing arguments built on a single case packet.

  • Attorney & witness roles with coaching
  • Case theory, evidence handling, and objections
  • Composure, clarity, and presence under pressure

Season at a Glance

  • Case release → training workshops
  • Scrimmages and invitationals
  • Regional competition
